Chandni has served healthy, delicious vegetarian food to the Santa Monica area for over 30 years.

From family recipes originating from Punjab northern India, the Kumar family's cuisine is created with fresh vegetables and spices that will have you returning for more. Chandni is abuzz for breakfast at daybreak, busy with a lunch buffet at noon, and delivers lovely dinners in the evening.
